About

I am a 4th year PhD candidate in Mathematics at Washington Univeristy in St. Louis (WUSTL). My advisor is Dr. Ari Stern. My research interests lie in geomertic numerical integration of ordinary differential equations. Currently, I serve as the president of the SIAM student chapter at WUSTL.

Prior to this, I completed my undergraudate degree in Mathematics and Computer Science at Grinnell College in Iowa.
